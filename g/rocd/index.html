RoCD - a roguelike

Remnant's of Chaotic Days is a roguelike inspired by various other titles already out on the market which has a few simple aims.

The main aims of the game will be to have the following major features:

  • Permadeath.
  • A procedurally generated open world.
  • Interesting stats, items and skills to lead to complex and strategic gameplay.
  • An interesting and though-provoking world story that is procedurally generated.
